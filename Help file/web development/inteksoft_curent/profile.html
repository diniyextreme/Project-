Who are we?
Inteksoft was established in 19 December 2005 at the point
when the IT industry in Malaysia was emerging swiftly.

We offer personalised ICT solutions and services for both public and private industries in the manufacturing, finance and service sectors. We operate as a catalyst in business process, re-engineering and building a competitive advantage in today's electronic world.

Our intellectual capability, business model and sustained geographic footprint is unsurpassed in the local market and we provide client with sophisticated and various service delivery options in the infrastructure, solutions, system integration and networking fields.

As one of the fastest growing ICT company in Malaysia, in terms of physical infrastructure and geographical footprint, We have the competency and ability to provide services to large organizations as well as small and medium sized entities.

Vision
To become the premier IT solutions provider & the leader in business consulting by providing value added services to every customer.
Mission
To create original, robust and flexible IT solutions that add value to customers' businesses by enabling them to grasp the opportunities offered by information technology and the Internet.
Our Recognitions
Inteksoft partners with leading vendors to provide the best solutions to our customers.
